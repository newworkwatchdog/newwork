Governed loop

The platform turns intelligence into controlled execution.

Every meaningful action moves through a repeatable execution loop before it changes a system or commits the enterprise.

01Context

Business data, systems, documents, policies, and owners are pulled into the work path.

02Policy

Roles, thresholds, permissions, and approval logic decide what the agent or person can do next.

03FLOW

The workflow routes tasks across people, agents, tools, and systems with the right sequence.

04Action

Agents and people complete the next step with the right authority and system access.

05Human control

Sensitive decisions, exceptions, and commitments are reviewed by the right person before they take effect.

06Evidence

Every decision, tool call, approval, and system update is captured for audit and improvement.
