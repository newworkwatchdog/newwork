Company

Founded in Berlin to close the AI execution gap.

NEWWORK was founded in Berlin in 2023 by Michael Jaszczyk and Jens Kruger to solve a problem they watched repeat across enterprise software implementations: AI that could advise but could not execute under governance.

Operating principles

Enterprise AI becomes valuable when it can execute under control.

NEWWORK is designed for enterprise buyers who need operational value, not isolated AI experiments.

Connect first

Enterprise buyers will not replace SAP, Workday, or Salesforce to adopt AI. NEWWORK integrates with existing systems of record rather than requiring migration, so AI execution works with the infrastructure already in place.

Govern by design

Production AI in regulated enterprises requires identity, policy, approval chains, and audit evidence before an action completes. NEWWORK builds governance into the execution fabric, not as an afterthought.

Execute real work

Advisors and copilots generate suggestions. NEWWORK completes traceable outcomes—approvals routed, invoices processed, onboarding workflows finished—with evidence that the work was done under control.

Scale through solutions

NEWWORK enters with packaged Value Packs that deliver operational value in weeks, then expands across the enterprise as buyers activate additional workflows on the same execution fabric.

Why we built this

The gap is not a training problem. It is an execution layer problem.

We founded NEWWORK because enterprises needed a different architecture. Systems of record were never designed to host AI execution, and AI platforms were never designed to enforce enterprise governance. The gap between what AI can do and what enterprises can deploy is not a training problem or a model problem—it is an execution layer problem. NEWWORK exists to close it.
